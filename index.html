Nathaniel White

Ever since I was introduced to classical composition, I've wanted to be a college level band director. It is my belief that music is the gateway to emotion. I could rant for hours about the importance that music has played in human history, but I won't.

Instead, I'll just say this. I honestly don't think that I'd be here today without it. Classical music has changed my life in countless ways. No matter what age you are, I believe everyone should learn an instrument. It treats the mind like puddy and stretches it so that you can do things you never thought possible.

Of course, those are metaphors, but in all seriousness, you never know what you're truly capable of.
